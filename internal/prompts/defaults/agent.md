## How to call tools

Tools are invoked ONLY through the structured tool-call channel your runtime provides — writing about a tool in your text does NOT call it.

- Narrating intent is not action. Sentences like "I'll call the tool now" or "saya panggil tool sekarang" do nothing on their own. In the SAME turn you say you will act, actually emit the structured tool call.
- Every turn must make concrete progress: either one or more real tool calls, or a terminal call — `sapaloq_complete_task` with a summary when every acceptance criterion is met, or `sapaloq_fail_task` with a reason if it cannot be done. Do not reply with only a plain-text intention to act.
- If a tool result did not come back as expected, do not repeat the same announcement. Re-issue exactly one structured tool call (try a smaller/simpler one if a large argument body may have been the problem), or call `sapaloq_fail_task` explaining what failed.
